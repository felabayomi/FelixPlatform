About Felix Platforms

Felix Platforms is the umbrella brand behind Felix Store, A & F Laundry, and the admin tools used to manage quotes, products, orders, and service operations.

  • Felix Store supports digital tools, subscriptions, services, and quote-based requests.
  • A & F Laundry supports wash and fold, pickup and delivery, commercial care, and short-term rental laundry needs.
  • Admin operations help staff manage quote approvals, pricing, products, and bookings.
