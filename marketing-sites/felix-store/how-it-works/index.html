HOW IT WORKS

How to Use Felix Store

Browse products and services, send a request, get pricing, and move forward once approved.

  1. Browse products and services in the store.
  2. Add items to your request list.
  3. Submit a quote request with your details.
  4. Our team reviews your request and confirms pricing.
  5. You receive a quote or invoice.
  6. Once approved, your order or service is scheduled or delivered.

Some products may be digital downloads, services, subscriptions, or consultations.
