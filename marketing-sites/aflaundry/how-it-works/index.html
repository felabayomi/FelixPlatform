How to Use A & F Laundry

Select a service, send your request, and receive pickup and delivery updates.

  1. Select a laundry service.
  2. Enter service details and pickup/delivery information.
  3. Submit a service request or quote request.
  4. We confirm pricing and schedule pickup.
  5. Laundry is processed and delivered back to you.
  6. You may also subscribe to recurring laundry services.
