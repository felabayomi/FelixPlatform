About A & F Laundry

A & F Laundry provides laundry pickup, wash and fold, dry cleaning, ironing, and delivery services for households, businesses, and short-term rental properties.

Customers can schedule pickups, request laundry services, and receive quotes through the app.

Laundry services may be priced per pound, per item, per load, or through subscription plans. Final pricing may be confirmed after review of the service request.
